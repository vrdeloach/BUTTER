Extra Info

Westwood Recreational Center

Our regularly scheduled games are all played at the Westwood Recreational Center.

Westwood hosts a variety of activities for people of all ages! Some of these include:

  • Open gym basketball every Tuesday, 6:30PM to 8PM (Free)
  • After school program for grade school children
  • Computer literacy classes
  • Community education classes
  • A ceramics studio
  • Fully featured exercise room, Monday through Friday, 8AM to 8PM (Free)
  • Sign up your children for other sports leagues such as JAWS soccer or church league basketball
  • Tennis lessons and workouts
  • Swimming lessons and open pool in the summertime

For more information about other activities at Westwood, visit their website.
