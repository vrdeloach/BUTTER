Board Info

The Advisory Board

The JCBL Advisory Board is a group of players who are passionate about what we stand for and who have an active role in the development of our league. The board makes or changes any league rules, organizes charity events, manages the intake of new players and teams, amongst many other important tasks. We are currently looking for new board members, so join today!

- Requirements to Join

  • Must be 18 years of age or older
  • Must have played at least one full season in JCBL
  • Must be willing to actively participate in charity events outside of JCBL
  • Must be able to attend monthly board meetings
  • Must fill out an application that you can request through the contact page or by visiting our office

- Board Member Perks

  • Have the first say when it comes to rule changes or league enhancements
  • $25 donated to charity of your choice upon acceptance
  • $25 credit for every new team recruited
  • Attendance at local charity events on behalf of JCBL
  • Priority game time requests
  • Many other great privileges, so apply as soon as you can!
